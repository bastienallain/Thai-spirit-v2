## The Best Rooftop Bars in Bangkok: An Elevated Experience

Bangkok’s skyline is best enjoyed with a cocktail in hand from the vantage point of a rooftop bar. Join Thai Spirit as we guide you to the most exhilarating rooftop experiences in Bangkok.

### Belga - Sofitel Bangkok Sukhumvit
![Belga](https://yourwebsite.com/path/to/belga.jpg)
- **Address**: 32/F, Sofitel Bangkok Sukhumvit, 189 Sukhumvit Road, Bangkok
- **Opening Hours**: Every day, 17:00 - 01:00
- **Specialty**: Belgian cuisine
- **View**: Breathtaking panorama of Bangkok
- **Note**: Belga is one of only nine establishments worldwide, and the only one in Southeast Asia, to serve Duvel on tap

### Above Eleven
![Above Eleven](https://yourwebsite.com/path/to/above-eleven.jpg)
- **Address**: Sukhumvit Soi 11, Bangkok 10110
- **Specialty**: Peruvian-Japanese fusion
- **View**: Overlooking Lumpini Park

### Sky Bar - Lebua at State Tower
![Sky Bar](https://yourwebsite.com/path/to/sky-bar.jpg)
- **Address**: The Dome at Lebua, Silom Rd, Bang Rak, Bangkok 10500, Thailand
- **Opening Hours**: 16:00 - 01:00
- **Highlights**: 
  - Featured in "The Hangover Part II"
  - Signature cocktail: Hangovertini
  - Average cocktail price: 800-1000 THB

### Octave Rooftop Lounge & Bar - Marriott Hotel Sukhumvit
![Octave Rooftop Lounge](https://yourwebsite.com/path/to/octave-rooftop.jpg)
- **Address**: 2 Sukhumvit Rd, Khwaeng Khlong Tan, Khet Khlong Toei, Bangkok 10110, Thailand
- **Opening Hours**: 17:00 - 02:00
- **Highlights**: 
  - 360-degree views of Bangkok
  - Happy hour from 17:00 to 19:00 daily
  - Average cocktail price: 400-600 THB

### Vertigo and Moon Bar - Banyan Tree Bangkok
![Vertigo and Moon Bar](https://yourwebsite.com/path/to/vertigo-moon-bar.jpg)
- **Address**: 21/100 South Sathon Road Sathon, Bangkok 10120, Thailand
- **Opening Hours**: 17:00 - 01:00
- **Highlights**: 
  - Views from 61 floors up
  - Dress code: Smart casual
  - Average cocktail price: 600-800 THB

### CRU Champagne Bar - Centara Grand at CentralWorld
![CRU Champagne Bar](https://yourwebsite.com/path/to/cru-champagne-bar.jpg)
- **Address**: 59th floor, Centara Grand at CentralWorld, Ratchadamri Road, Pathumwan, Bangkok
- **Opening Hours**: 17:00 - 01:00
- **Highlights**: 
  - Spectacular views of the Bangkok horizon
  - Premium drinks with G.H. Mumm Champagne
  - Cocktail recommendations: La Vie En Rose and Swallow’s Nest

### Alfresco 64 – A Chivas Bar - Lebua at State Tower
![Alfresco 64](https://yourwebsite.com/path/to/alfresco-64.jpg)
- **Address**: 64th floor, The Dome at State Tower, Silom Road, Bangkok
- **Opening Hours**: 17:00 - 01:00
- **Highlights**:
  - The world’s highest whisky bar
  - Exclusive Chivas blends
  - Offers a luxurious yacht-themed setting with unparalleled views of Bangkok

### Bar.Yard - Kimpton Maa-Lai Bangkok
![Bar.Yard](https://yourwebsite.com/path/to/bar-yard.jpg)
- **Address**: Kimpton Maa-Lai Bangkok, 78 Soi Tonson, Bangkok, 10330
- **Opening Hours**: Open Tue-Sun 16:00-24:00
- **Price Range**: 2 of 4
- **Highlights**:
  - Situated on the 40th floor, offering breathtaking views of the Sathorn skyline
  - Instagram-friendly space with vibrant colors and lush greenery designed by the renowned architectural design firm Hassell
  - Varied menu with options such as Slow-cooked Chipotle Beef Briskets (B360) and Oven-dried Wagyu Butchers Steak Skewers (B320)
  - Tiki-style cocktails including the rum-based Navy Grog (B310) and the tequila-based The Queen’s Group Pineapple (B320)
  - Value for money, debunking the notion that rooftop dining in a swanky hotel would be excessively expensive

### Brewski - Radisson Blu Plaza Bangkok
![Brewski](https://yourwebsite.com/path/to/brewski.jpg)
- **Address**: 30th floor, Radisson Blu Plaza Bangkok Hotel, Soi Sukhumvit 27, Bangkok
- **Cross Street**: Sukhumvit
- **Opening Hours**: 17:00-1:00
- **Highlights**:
  - Currently the highest beer bar in Bangkok
  - Located on the 30th floor of Radisson Blu Plaza Bangkok Hotel
  - 12 rotating draft beers from around the world on tap
  - Stunning skyline views of Bangkok

### Cooling Tower Rooftop Bar - Carlton Hotel Bangkok Sukhumvit
![Cooling Tower Rooftop Bar](https://yourwebsite.com/path/to/coolingtower.jpg)
- **Address**: Carlton Hotel Bangkok Sukhumvit, Bangkok
- **Opening Hours**: Wed-Sun 17:00-24:00
- **Neighborhood**: Watthana
- **Highlights**:
  - Situated 34 floors above Sukhumvit, offering sweeping views of Bangkok's midtown
  - Happy hour offer: buy-one-get-one-free on selected drinks (5:30 pm - 7:30 pm)
  - Rooftop dining and wining venue of the Carlton Hotel

### Flûte A Perrier-Jouët Bar - Le Bua
![Flûte A Perrier-Jouët Bar](https://yourwebsite.com/path/to/flute.jpg)
- **Address**: 64th floor, The Dome at State Tower, Silom Road, Bangkok, 10500
- **Neighborhood**: Charoenkrung
- **Price**: 4 of 4
- **Highlights**:
- Located on the 64th floor of the State Tower Building
- The world's highest outdoor Champagne bar
- Exclusive champagne selection only available at this venue
- Limited seating for an exclusive experience
- Elegant design catering to a luxurious taste

### The Loft - Waldorf Astoria Bangkok
![The Loft](https://yourwebsite.com/path/to/theloft.jpg)
- **Address**: 56th floor, Waldorf Astoria Bangkok, 151 Ratchadamri Road, Bangkok
- **Neighborhood**: Ratchadamri
- **Price**: 4 of 4
- **Highlights**:
- Situated on the 56th floor, offering expansive views of Bangkok
- Inspired by the classic "The Old Waldorf-Astoria Bar Book"
- Creative cocktails crafted by renowned bar manager Michele Montauti
- Elegant and artistic interior evoking a cool artist's studio
- Features big communal wooden tables and Art Nouveau elements
