## Introduction

Bangkok, Thailand's vibrant capital, is famed for its majestic skyscrapers and breathtaking views. What better way to enjoy the beautiful city than from one of its best rooftop bars?

## Must-Visit Bars

### Sky Bar - Lebua at State Tower
#### Address: The Dome at Lebua, Silom Rd, Bang Rak, Bangkok 10500, Thailand
#### Opening Hours: 16:00 - 01:00
- Known for its stunning panoramic views and being featured in the movie "The Hangover Part II."
- Signature cocktail: Hangovertini
- The average cost of a cocktail: 800-1000 THB

### Octave Rooftop Lounge & Bar - Marriott Hotel Sukhumvit
#### Address: 2 Sukhumvit Rd, Khwaeng Khlong Tan, Khet Khlong Toei, Krung Thep Maha Nakhon 10110, Thailand
#### Opening Hours: 17:00 - 02:00
- This bar offers 360-degree views of Bangkok.
- Happy hour: From 17:00 to 19:00 daily
- The average cost of a cocktail: 400-600 THB

### Vertigo and Moon Bar - Banyan Tree Bangkok
#### Address: 21/100 South Sathon Road Sathon, Bangkok 10120, Thailand
#### Opening Hours: 17:00 - 01:00
- Located 61 floors up, it offers breathtaking views of the city.
- Dress code: Smart casual
- The average cost of a cocktail: 600-800 THB

### CRU Champagne Bar - Centara Grand at CentralWorld
#### Address: 999/99 Rama 1 Road, Phatumwan, Bangkok 10330, Thailand
#### Opening Hours: 17:00 - 01:00
- Known for having a wide selection of champagnes.
- Signature dish: Fine de Claire oysters
- The average cost of a cocktail: 700-900 THB


## Tips for a Great Evening

- **Reservation**: Remember to book your table in advance, especially during the high season.
- **Dress Code**: Most rooftop bars have a dress code. Make sure to check this before heading to the location.
- **Arrival Time**: Try to arrive just before sunset to catch the magnificent view.
